About Our Solution

The Smart Intelligent Traffic Orchestrator (SITO)

As railway network congestion and operational complexity grow, the traditional manual approach to train traffic control faces significant limitations. There is a critical need for an intelligent, data-driven system to enhance the efficiency, punctuality, and overall utilization of the existing railway infrastructure.

SITO is a decision-support system that acts as a co-pilot for section controllers. It uses a powerful combination of AI, Operations Research, and Reinforcement Learning to optimize section throughput and provide real-time guidance for conflict-free, safe, and efficient train operations.
